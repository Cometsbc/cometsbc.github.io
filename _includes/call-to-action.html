When is it!

It is a sociable but competitive badminton club which was established in 1979 at Gosling Stadium in Welwyn and moved to Hertfordshire Sports Village (HSV) in Hatfield, 5 mins from Stablans, in 2003. We currently operate at three different times:

  • Senior club (Thursdays 8-10pm, Sundays 7-9pm. Matches: Tuesdays 7:30-10:30pm)
  • Junior club (Sundays 6-7pm)
  • Adult development club (Fridays 7:30-8:30pm)
