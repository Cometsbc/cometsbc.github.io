Who are we?

Comets are the largest and one of the most successful badminton clubs playing in the Hertfordshire County Leagues. We have nearly 50 members and 6 teams in the county leagues, with Ladies, Mixed and Mens teams all represented in the top division. We pride ourselves on our competitive yet friendly atmosphere, with a strong social side to the club away from the badminton. .

  • Throughout the year, club nights run twice a week on Thursdays (8-10pm) and Sundays (7pm to 9pm).
  • Due to recent popularity, the club is nearly at full capacity. We are therefore only currently considering the strongest players as new members.
